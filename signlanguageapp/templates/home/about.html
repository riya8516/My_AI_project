About Our Project

The system detects hand gestures in real time using your webcam and predicts sign language alphabets with high accuracy. Sign Language AI is an intelligent communication system that converts hand gestures into readable text using Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, TensorFlow, OpenCV and MediaPipe.

The main objective of this project is to reduce the communication gap between hearing-impaired people and others by providing accurate real-time sign language recognition. Our mission is to help bridge the communication gap between hearing-impaired people and everyone else using Artificial Intelligence.
